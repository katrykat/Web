About me

I started my career as a graphic designer at the school, doing some designs for the taxi companies "Corp taxi" and "Taxi Los cortijos" in my city, after that I started the university obtaining a degree in Marketing and advertising. At the moment I was working as Graphic Designer for a small company where I had the opportunity to do some work for large companies associated with Polar and Cocacola.

I started to focus my career on Web Design in 2013 when due to a Community Manager course I did a blog using wordpress, it was then that I learned part of the process of a web page. I am currently working for a large company where I have to design, update or migrate web pages for companies like Canon, Starbucks and Microsoft among others.
